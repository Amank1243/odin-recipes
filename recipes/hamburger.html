This is a Hamburger

Description

To make a hamburger, start by shaping ground beef into a patty, seasoning both sides with salt and pepper. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the patty for about 3â€“4 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. While the patty is cooking, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster. Once the patty is done, place it on the bottom bun and top it with cheese if desired, allowing it to melt slightly from the heat. Add your favorite toppings like lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments such as ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise. Finish by placing the top bun on, and your homemade hamburger is ready to enjoy!

  • Sliced cheese
  • 1/2lb patty of angus beef

  • Mayo
