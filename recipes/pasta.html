The dish is called Pasta

Description

To make a simple garlic and olive oil pasta (Aglio e Olio), start by bringing a large pot of salted water to a boil and cooking 200g of spaghetti (or your preferred pasta) until al dente. While the pasta cooks, heat 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil in a pan over medium heat, then add 3–4 thinly sliced garlic cloves and cook until they’re golden and fragrant, being careful not to burn them. Stir in about 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes for a bit of heat. Once the pasta is done, reserve about 1/2 cup of the cooking water before draining it. Add the pasta to the pan with the garlic oil and toss to coat, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water if needed to loosen it up. Finish with some chopped fresh parsley, a sprinkle of grated Parmesan or Pecorino, and a squeeze of lemon juice if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 3-4 cloves of Garlic
  • Salt
  • Red Chilie Flakes
  • Tbsp of Lemon Juice
