This is a Sandwhich

Description

To make a sandwich, start by choosing your bread—sliced white, wheat, rye, or even a roll—then lay two slices flat on a clean surface. Spread your condiments on the inside of each slice, like mayo, mustard, or hummus, depending on your preference. Next, layer on your main fillings, such as deli meats, cheese, or a plant-based alternative. Add any extras you like: lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, or even avocado. Once everything’s stacked to your liking, place the second slice of bread on top, press gently to hold it all together, and, if desired, cut the sandwich in half for easier eating. Enjoy it as is, or toast or grill it for added flavor and crunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Sliced cheese
  • Deli cut meat
  • Lettuce
  • Mayo

    • Steps

    1. Place one piece of bread on the counter
    2. Stack ingredients ontop of each other
    3. Enjoy!
